About

Team Composition

Columbia Chinese Soccer Team is a well-deserved outstanding team in the East Coast which was founded and developed by Columbia University alumni in 2009. The current team consists of three parts: management layer (7 team committee, 5 managers), players (40 students, 70 graduates) and fans (more than 100).

Brief Introduction

We organize 1-2 training sessions every week, and participate in friendly matches and small competitions. Our team has won impressive results in Ivy Cup, East Coast Cup, Meiguan Cup and Alumni Cup which have been reported by Chinese American media.Our team has a high reputation in Columbia and the soccer community in New York.
