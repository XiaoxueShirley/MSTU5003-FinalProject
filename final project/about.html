ABOUT

Columbia Chinese Soccer Team is a well-deserved outstanding team in the East Coast which was founded and developed by Columbia University alumni in 2009. The current team consists of three parts: management layer (7 team committee, 5 managers), players (40 students, 70 graduates) and fans (more than 100). We organize 1-2 training sessions every week, and participate in friendly matches about 4 times per semester, small competitions, etc. Our team has won impressive results in Ivy Cup, East Coast Cup, Meiguan Cup and Alumni Cup which have been reported by Chinese American media. In addition, we also own multiple social media platforms for public communication.Our team has a high reputation in Columbia and the soccer community in New York.
